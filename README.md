The Digital Barangay Blotter Management System is a web-based platform developed specifically for the barangay officials of Barangay Banay-Banay 2nd, San Jose, Batangas. The primary objective of this software is to transition barangay incident management from a manual, paper-based logbook into a centralized digital platform.Beyond standard record-keeping, the system serves as a powerful analytical tool designed to support decision-making. It enables the classification of incidents by danger level and provides comprehensive insights, including the average duration for case resolution. To ensure data integrity and advanced spatial analysis, the system integrates the following intelligent features:Duplicate Detection: Utilizes the Levenshtein Distance algorithm to identify and consolidate highly similar or duplicate reports from the same location.Geospatial Clustering: Employs the DBSCAN (Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise) algorithm to dynamically generate heatmaps and visualize high-density incident zones.Trend Analytics: Generates monthly and yearly trend reports to help officials identify crime patterns and correlate incidents with specific community factors.
